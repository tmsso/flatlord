6 · RATIONALE
Accent: deep teal

Confident without being "default Office". Keeps semantic blue free for info, harmonises with warm greys (teal is their complement), and holds AA as a button fill in light and as text-on-dark. All three options ship as one-line token swaps — flip above to compare.

Type: IBM Plex Sans

A grotesque literally designed for enterprise clarity; full Hungarian set incl. double-acute ő/ű, true tabular figures (tnum) for money and meter columns, 400–700 weights for honest hierarchy, and a matching Mono for serials and token names. No ALL-CAPS anywhere.

Density: two registers

Admin is comfortable-dense: 36px rows, 13px table text, 16–20px card padding — sheet-like scanning without cramping. Tenant is airy: 44px+ touch targets, 48px list rows, one primary action per screen. Same tokens, two spacing registers.
